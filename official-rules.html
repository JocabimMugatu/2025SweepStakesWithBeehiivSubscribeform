AMOE — Free Entry (no purchase): Receive the same number of entries without a purchase by mailing a legible, hand‑printed postcard containing: your full name, mailing address, email, phone, and the specific game(s) and entry tier(s) you want (choose one per game: 20×, 50×, or 100×; you may also request “Season Pass (20× per game)” for all 12 games). Mail to: Chief Brief Sweepstakes AMOE, Brief Media LLC, 403 Stadium Dr, Tallahassee, FL 32304. Postcards must be received at least three (3) days before the applicable drawing date; one AMOE request per person per day. Illegible, incomplete or bulk/automated submissions are void.
